What

Robotics is a technological field that provides products that reduce man-hours required to perform a task, that reduce human risk in hazardous environments, and that reduces economic cost per task but currently provides a lower level of reliability than alternatives. The proper working of robotic systems often involves sensors, embedded processors, actuators, and communication devices for reactive control and/or data analysis.
